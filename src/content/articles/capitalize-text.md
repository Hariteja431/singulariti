## Why You Need a Capitalize Text

Formatting raw, uncapitalized transcripts manually is slow.

## How Capitalize Text Works

The engine identifies sentence borders and capitalizes leading characters for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

## When to Use This Tool

Use the **Capitalize Text** when you need to capitalize the first letter of each sentence in text blocks during cleaning video transcripts, formatting essays, and drafting emails.

### How does it find sentences?

It looks for periods, question marks, and exclamation marks followed by spaces to detect sentence breaks.
