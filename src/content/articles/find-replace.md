### Advanced Editing Options

To give you complete control over your edits, we offer vital toggle settings:
*   **Match Case:** By default, find and replace tools are case-insensitive (finding both "Apple" and "apple"). By enabling "Match Case," the tool will only find exact matches of the capitalization you entered, preventing accidental alterations of proper nouns or specific code syntax.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Can I replace a word with a blank space?
Yes! Simply type the word you want to remove into the "Find" box and leave the "Replace" box completely empty. The tool will delete every instance of the "Find" word from your text.

### Is there a file size limit?
Because the tool processes text locally in your browser, there is no strict file size limit imposed by us. You can paste very large documents, though incredibly massive files (millions of words) may cause older browsers to slow down momentarily during the replacement process.

### Does the tool change the original formatting like bold or italics?
The tool operates on plain text. If you paste text from a word processor that contains rich formatting (bold, italics, underlines), that formatting will likely be lost. It is designed primarily for editing raw text, code, and unformatted data.

### Can I find and replace line breaks or paragraph returns?
Currently, this specific tool focuses on standard alphanumeric character strings. Advanced regular expression (Regex) replacements for line breaks require specialized coding environments, but standard text, symbols, and spacing are handled perfectly.

### Is my sensitive data safe?
Absolutely. We recognize that users often edit sensitive business documents or proprietary code. Because the script runs client-side, your data never leaves your computer. It is 100% secure.
