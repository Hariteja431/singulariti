## Why You Need a Find and Replace Text

Manually updating a repeated name or word in a large draft takes time.

## How Find and Replace Text Works

The engine applies global regex replacement functions to text blocks client-side for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

## When to Use This Tool

Use the **Find and Replace Text** when you need to locate and replace words in text during renaming character logs, cleaning drafts, and fixing typo arrays.
