## Why You Need a PDF Image Extractor

Saving specific photographs or illustrations embedded inside a PDF page-by-page manually is slow and degrades image resolution.

## How PDF Image Extractor Works

The extractor parses PDF binary directories to isolate embedded image streams and downloads them client-side in their original formats.

### Can I extract images from scanned PDFs?

If the PDF consists of scanned pages, each full page is treated as an image and can be extracted.
