## Why You Need a Image Upscaler

Blowing up small graphics using standard software results in jagged pixel borders.

## How Image Upscaler Works

The engine applies bilinear or bicubic interpolation algorithms client-side for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

Use the **Image Upscaler** when you need to enlarge small images using canvas smoothing filters during enlarging website icons, cleaning low-res clips, and prepping graphics for print.
