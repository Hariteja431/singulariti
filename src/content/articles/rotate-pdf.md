## How Rotate PDF Works

The engine updates the rotation metadata flags in the PDF catalog structure for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

## When to Use This Tool

Use the **Rotate PDF** when you need to rotate PDF document pages by 90, 180, or 270 degrees during correcting sideways scans, turning landscape charts, and preparing files.
