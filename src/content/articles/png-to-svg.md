## Why You Need a PNG to SVG

Raster PNGs pixelate when scaled up; vector SVGs can be enlarged infinitely without losing quality.

## How PNG to SVG Works

The engine traces raster pixel boundaries using local image vectorization scripts for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

## When to Use This Tool

Use the **PNG to SVG** when you need to convert PNG raster images into scalable SVG vector graphics during vectorizing logos, creating scalable illustrations, and converting low-res scans.

### Can I vectorize a photo?

Tracing photos results in massive files with thousands of complex paths. The tool works best for logos, shapes, and text designs.
