## Why You Need a Certificate Editor

Creating professional certificates for students, employees, or event attendees requires flexible template editing without expensive design software.

## How Certificate Editor Works

The browser-based application reads templates and user content in-memory. The engine uses Fabric.js client-side canvas rendering to support custom background images, text field editing, drawing paths/signatures, drag-and-drop alignment, and outputs high-quality PDFs or images securely.

## When to Use This Tool

Use the **Certificate Editor** when you need to design, customize, or bulk-edit achievement certificates, diplomas, or course completion awards using any local file template path.

## Step-by-Step Usage

1. **Select template or upload image**: Select a pre-designed certificate template or upload your own template from any file path.
2. **Add text fields**: Add custom text fields (Name, Title, Date) and position them anywhere on the canvas.
3. **Customize and sign**: Draw or upload your signature, place logos/seals, and style fonts and colors.
4. **Download**: Download your completed certificate as a print-ready PDF or high-resolution PNG.

## Practical Example

For example, using the Certificate Editor to customize course certificates locally, overlaying student names and instructor signatures entirely in your browser without uploading private details to any third-party server.

## Advantages

- **Local Processing**: No data leaves your device — completely client-side and secure.
- **Visual Drag & Drop**: Drag-and-drop text positioning and custom text path options.
- **Custom Assets**: Supports drawing signatures and importing external transparent PNG logos.
- **Print Quality**: Export as vector-quality PDF or high-resolution raster images.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Using low-resolution images as custom templates, causing blurry certificate printouts.

## Privacy and Safe Usage

Since all rendering occurs inside your browser memory, your templates, logos, and signatures are never uploaded to any server. Make sure to download your completed files before closing the page, as progress is not saved.

## FAQs

### Can I use my own certificate template design?

Yes. You can upload any JPEG or PNG image from your device to use as a custom background template.

### Does this tool store my custom certificates?

No. All edits are processed entirely in your browser memory. Make sure to download your files, as they are not stored on any server.
