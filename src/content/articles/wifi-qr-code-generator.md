## Why You Need a Wi-Fi QR Code Generator

## How Wi-Fi QR Code Generator Works

The engine encodes Wi-Fi format tags `WIFI:S:SSID;T:WPA;P:PASSWORD;;` into QR grids for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

## When to Use This Tool

Use the **Wi-Fi QR Code Generator** when you need to create scannable QR codes containing Wi-Fi access credentials during sharing customer Wi-Fi networks in cafes, restaurants, and offices.
