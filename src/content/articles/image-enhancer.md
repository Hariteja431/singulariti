## Why You Need a Image Enhancer

Photos are often dark, washed out, or lack color pop when captured in low light.

## How Image Enhancer Works

The engine applies histogram equalization and contrast filters client-side for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

## When to Use This Tool

Use the **Image Enhancer** when you need to auto-enhance image lighting and color balance during improving photo exposure, brightening dark shots, and editing blog graphics.
