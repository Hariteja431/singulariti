## Why You Need a Data Storage Converter

Hard drive manufacturers measure in base-10, while operating systems measure in base-2, creating size discrepancies.

## How Data Storage Converter Works

## FAQs

### Why is a 1TB hard drive shown as 931GB in Windows?

Drive makers define 1TB as 1,000,000,000,000 bytes. Windows calculates in binary base-1024, resulting in 931.3GB.
