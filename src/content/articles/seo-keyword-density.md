## FAQs

### What is heading hierarchy?

It is the structural tree of headings on a page (H1 followed by H2, H3, etc.). Keeping headings in chronological order helps search engines understand your content layout.

### Does generating meta tags improve search rankings?

Meta tags don't guarantee rankings, but clean titles and compelling descriptions increase click-through rates (CTR) from search result pages.
