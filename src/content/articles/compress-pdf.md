## Why You Need a Compress PDF

PDF documents with many images are often too heavy for email attachments and portal upload limits.

## How Compress PDF Works

The engine compresses embedded images and downscales vector resolutions client-side for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.
