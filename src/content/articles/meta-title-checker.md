## FAQs

### Does generating meta tags improve search rankings?

Meta tags don't guarantee rankings, but clean titles and compelling descriptions increase click-through rates (CTR) from search result pages.

### How long should my meta descriptions be?

Keep them between 120 and 155 characters. This ensures that the snippet is not truncated on search engine results pages.
