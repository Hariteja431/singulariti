## Why You Need a JPG to SVG

JPG logos blur when stretched; converting to SVG allows infinite scaling for billboards or responsive webs.

## How JPG to SVG Works

The engine traces JPG pixel matrices to generate vector equations client-side for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

## FAQs

### Will the SVG look identical?

Tracing converts pixel grids to flat color regions. It will look like a stylized or vectorized version of the original image.
