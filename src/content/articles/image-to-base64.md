### Can I convert multiple images at once?

Yes, you can upload and batch convert multiple images. Once processed, you can download all converted files in one go.

### Why is WebP recommended for websites?

WebP offers superior compression compared to JPG and PNG, yielding file sizes that are 25% to 30% smaller at equivalent visual quality, improving page load speeds.
