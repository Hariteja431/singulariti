## Why You Need a SVG to WebP

Sharing vector assets on websites is sometimes risky due to security vulnerabilities in raw SVG XML; WebP is a secure, optimized alternative.

## How SVG to WebP Works

The engine rasterizes SVG curves and exports them as optimized WebP image blobs for high-speed operation, providing instant feedback without sending any data over the internet.

## FAQs

### Is WebP vector-based?

No. WebP is a raster format, meaning the vector SVG is converted into a grid of pixels.
